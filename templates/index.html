What is PeerPort?

With PeerPort, you can monetize your next trip.


Shoppers in Ghana and Nigeria have difficulty buying items from Amazon.com and other stores in the US and Europe due to high shipping costs.


Travelling soon? Why not help someone bring some items while you make some money doing so?

Sign up to be a Jetter

How It Works

Jetter searches for available gigs.
Jetter & Shopper negotiate & agree on the delivery fee.
Shopper's payment for item(s) & delivery are held in escrow
Jetter pays for item with their own fun.
Jetter arrives at rendezvous point and delivers item(s)
Full item amount & delivery fee are released to Jetter.
